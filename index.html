About the resort

Where families come
together, and memories
come to life.

Located along Multan Road, approximately 20 minutes from Thokar Niaz Baig, The Oasis Golf & Aqua Resort is Lahore's premier members-only haven — a place where world-class sport, leisure, and family entertainment coexist across a sweeping landscape designed to refresh the spirit.

With serene surroundings and a diverse range of invigorating facilities, the resort gives you and your family a perfect blend of tranquility and adventure.
